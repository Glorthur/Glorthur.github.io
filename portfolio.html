Retail Customer Segmentation Analysis

SQL Tableau Python

Business Challenge

RetailCo needed to optimize their marketing strategy based on customer behavior. The key question was: How can we identify distinct customer segments to better target marketing campaigns?

Analytical Approach

I approached this challenge by:

  • Collecting and cleaning transaction data from the company's CRM and sales database
  • Using K-means clustering to identify distinct customer segments based on purchase frequency, recency and monetary value
  • Creating interactive dashboards highlighting buying patterns across different segments

Key Findings & Impact

The analysis revealed four distinct customer segments: high-value loyalists, occasional big spenders, frequent small purchasers, and at-risk customers. These findings enabled the marketing team to develop targeted campaigns for each segment, resulting in a 23% increase in campaign response rates and 15% improvement in customer retention.
