Featured Case Study

Retail Sales Analysis & Forecasting

1

Problem Definition

A retail client needed to understand seasonal sales patterns and improve inventory management. They were experiencing stockouts during peak periods and excess inventory during slow periods.

2

Data Collection & Cleaning

Gathered 3 years of sales data across 12 store locations. Cleaned missing values, standardized product categories, and merged multiple data sources using SQL and Python.

SELECT 
    date, store_id, 
    SUM(sales_amount) as daily_sales,
    COUNT(DISTINCT transaction_id) as transaction_count
FROM sales_transactions
WHERE date BETWEEN '2022-01-01' AND '2024-12-31'
GROUP BY date, store_id
ORDER BY date, store_id;

Analysis & Visualization
